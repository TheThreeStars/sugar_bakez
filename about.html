Welcome to Sugar Bakez

Hey! I’m Faraah and welcome to Sugar Bakez where every bite is crafted with love, passion, and lots of happiness! I am a small home-based bakery specializing in decadent variations of brownies, also offering cookies, cakes, and lemon loaves.

What started as a passion project to a full-time university student, has blossomed into a sweet venture dedicated to spreading joy one treat at a time. Baking has always been my love language—a way to connect, share, and bring a little sweetness into the lives of others. Each creation is made with the finest ingredients and a whole lot of care, ensuring that every product is not only delicious but made to make you smile. You’ll always find something to satisfy your sweet tooth with us.

Thank you for supporting my dream—I’m so excited to share my love of baking with you!

With love,
Faarah
